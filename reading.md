<h2>Super Topics</h2>

I will read anything written about a few topics. If you know of a book or body of work on one of these subjects, please let me know.

<strong>The Apollo Program</strong>
I will never tire of reading about the incredible feat of science and cooperation that led to man’s successful landing on the moon. My favorite books on it are:
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Apollo-Race-Moon-Charles-Murray/dp/0671611011/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&amp;keywords=apollo+charles+murray&amp;qid=1619539952&amp;s=books&amp;sr=1-1">Apollo</a> (if you read no other book about Apollo, read this one)
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Digital-Apollo-Human-Machine-Spaceflight/dp/0262516101">Digital Apollo</a>
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Flight-My-Life-Mission-Control/dp/0525945717/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&amp;keywords=chris+craft+apollo&amp;qid=1619540050&amp;s=audible&amp;sr=1-1-catcorr">Flight</a>
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Rocket-Men-Robert-Kurson-audiobook/dp/B075H2P225/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&amp;keywords=apollo+program&amp;qid=1619540027&amp;s=audible&amp;sr=1-1">Rocket Men</a>

<h2>Fiction</h2>

At night I read mostly science fiction. I believe these series represent a likely near future path for humanity. I’ve gained new lessons and perspectives each time I’ve re-read these books. And they are dangerously fun to read when you're trying to go to sleep.
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Nexus-Trilogy-Book-1-ebook/dp/B00TOZI7FM/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&amp;keywords=nexus+book+one&amp;qid=1619565489&amp;s=books&amp;sr=1-1">Nexus</a> (the entire trilogy…this is my favorite book ever)
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Ready-Player-One-Ernest-Cline-audiobook/dp/B005HG7BWC/ref=sr_1_3?dchild=1&amp;keywords=ready+player+one&amp;qid=1619565502&amp;s=books&amp;sr=1-3">Ready Player One</a> and <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08C7DQWRC?plink=Trw1BcbPjxdtgoYO&amp;ref=adblp13nvvxx_0_0_im">Two</a>
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00GIUG3ES/ref=dp-kindle-redirect?_encoding=UTF8&amp;btkr=1">Red Rising</a>. This entire series is captivating but I don’t recommend it for bedtime, it’s too suspenseful. Reaper forever!
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0038QN2AS/ref=dp-kindle-redirect?_encoding=UTF8&amp;btkr=1">Daemon Series</a> An extremely precient illustration of a very believable near future.

Unrelated
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07HJV1XBB/ref=dp-kindle-redirect?_encoding=UTF8&amp;btkr=1">On Earth We’re Briefly Gorgeous</a>. This is the only book that’s ever made me cry on the last page.
