My friend and partner Tony Hsieh encouraged me to read this book after a dinner discussion about internal vs external orientation. The book studies the concept of givers and how their success compares to takers. Of all the great points in it the one that I come back to daily is that if it takes less than five minutes to help someone, always say yes.
